New Development: Ghost

 

Follow a lost and forgotten soul, Ghost, as he roams around a barren village. While his adventure may look cute and charming, his past is quiet the opposite. Uncover who Ghost was and the dark secrets of his history. This thought-provoking interactive storytelling game is bound to change your perspective of those forgotten in the after life.

Do you often have better things to do? Well then this is the perfect game for you! FAQ

How much does it cost to play your games?

All of our current releases are free to play, including the Ghost demo. The full release of Ghost may include a price tag, but we promise it won’t be too much!

Why the name “Purple Dog”?

Purple is a great color and dogs are great animals! Sorry there’s not a better answer.

I love the style of your games, do you sell the assets?

We do sell pixel-based assets through our itch.io page here.

It is still up in the air about selling our assets to Ghost at this time. If and when they become available, you will see an announcement on our website.

If you are ever interested in having us create custom assets for your game, please contact us here. We’d be happy to discuss details and pricing privately!

Help! I have a question about my recent merchandise purchase.

Unfortunately Purple Dog is not responsible for the production, shipping, and handling of merchandise. Please contact our supplier here.

Do you have any career opportunities?

Currently we do not have any paid career opportunities. However, if you are looking to collaborate or assist with our newest developments, we’d love to have you!

Please reach out to us through our Contact page.
