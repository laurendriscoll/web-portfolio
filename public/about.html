WHY DESIGN?

Creativity has always been an important part of me. Throughout high school I experimented with illustration, character design, animation, 3D modeling, and more! It wasn't until I learned how to code that I discovered that the design industry is where I belong. With my competitive attitude and passion for learning, I know that this industry is where I will succeed.

EXPERIENCE AND EDUCATION

Currently I am attending Baldwin Wallace University as an Interactive Design major with a minor in Marketing. I had an emphasis in Cybersecurity during my first year. My goal with my education is to develop and challenge my skills in user experience, branding, and design. I will be graduating in May of 2023.
