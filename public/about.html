ABOUT ME

WHY DESIGN?

Long story short: I always knew I needed to pursue a creative field. I knew it wouldn’t be easy and I took steps as a kid to make reaching this goal easier. I actually shocked my family and teachers by ‘wasting’ my intelligence to pursue art and I spent a lot of time questioning if it would be worth it. Truth is, I enjoy nothing more than creating. I specifically chose design because it was the best fit for my skills and mindset, but this wasn’t an easy or quick realization for me. If you’d like to learn more about my passion, please check out my story below.

EXPERIENCE AND EDUCATION

I am an Interactive Design major with a minor in Marketing and an early emphasis in Computer Science. I say that last part because I was also a Cybersecurity major for a year and I have learned a lot from my time in the major. As far as time and credits, I am a second year junior. I still plan to graduate in 4 years, which would be in May of 2023.

Additionally, I work in IT for the Shaker Heights City School district. I do not have any formal experience in the design field, but I am looking to change that. That being said, don’t underestimate my ability due to my lack of ‘experience’. I spend nearly all of my free time refining my creative skills. I recommend you check out my projects, and pay attention to the dates that I mention. I learn and improve very quickly - and I plan to continue to do so.
